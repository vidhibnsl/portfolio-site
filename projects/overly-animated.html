Overly Animated — The Pitch

Experience

Leading the design, cinematics and renders for the pitch "Overly Animated" to be held at PHNTM's Volume. Lead artist for all aspects.

"Overly Animated" defines the use of mocap and virtual production technology for live events, specifically comedy. Comedians' movements get animated on the screen behind them as characters designed by the team. Tools used: Unreal Engine, Maya, Blender.
