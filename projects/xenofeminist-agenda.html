Xenofeminist Agenda

Cinematic · Experimental Film

A visual translation of the Xenofeminist Manifesto using Unity and Unreal Engine.

Role — Concept, Designer, Lead Environment Artist

The Xenofeminist Manifesto by Laboria Cuboniks is a fierce corruption of the world we live in today, in the best sense of the word. It aspires a way of thinking that co-relates directly to define a radical change in a world that is so astute with its customs.

We used Unreal Engine to translate the written version of the manifesto to a visual one, one that tries to define the potency of change that the manifesto suggests, while retaining its open authenticity. This is our interpretation of the Xeno feminist manifesto.
